Let's Talk About Modularity

Modularity is a word worn thin by overuse. What it actually means in the Anyplace system.

Beyond Building Blocks: Unleashing Unlimited Design with Anyplace Modular Construction

In the ever-evolving world of construction, the demand for efficiency, adaptability, and sustainability has never been greater. Traditional building methods, while time-tested, often fall short in addressing these critical needs, leading to significant waste, limited flexibility, and a considerable environmental footprint.

Enter Anyplace Modular Construction – a revolutionary system born from a decade of dedicated research and development, poised to redefine how we build and interact with our spaces. At its core lies a powerful combination: intelligent Design for Industrialization (DfIND), a commitment to material evolution (championing the potential of aluminium), and an inherent modularity that unlocks truly unlimited design possibilities.

The Power of Simple Connections: Understanding Anyplace Modularity

Imagine building with oversized, intelligent Lego blocks. That's the essence of Anyplace's modularity. Our system utilizes a straightforward yet robust bolted connection method, allowing individual 15sqm modules to be seamlessly joined together. The principle is beautifully simple: 1 + 1 = 2. Just as two individual blocks combine to form a larger one, two 15sqm Anyplace modules can be effortlessly connected to create a spacious 30sqm area.

But the ingenuity doesn't stop there. The internal walls of these joining modules are designed for easy removal, transforming two distinct units into a single, unified space. This inherent flexibility empowers users to adapt their living or working environments as their needs evolve. Need an extra room? Simply bolt on another module. Find yourself with more space than required? The modular nature allows for potential resale or repurposing of individual units, fostering a circular economy of parts rather than landfill waste.

Hybrid Construciton - Blending old with new by design.

Stepping Beyond the Module: The Beauty of Hybrid Construction

While Anyplace shines as a standalone modular system, its true potential is amplified by its designed compatibility for hybrid construction with traditional timber and steel structures. This opens up a realm of truly unlimited possibilities for architects, builders, and homeowners alike. Hybrid construction, in this context, means seamlessly integrating Anyplace modules with the established strengths and aesthetics of conventional building materials.

Imagine the design freedom this unlocks:

  • Blending Efficiency with Warmth: Utilizing the rapid assembly and adaptability of Anyplace modules for the core structure of a home, while incorporating timber framing for stunning visual accents, natural warmth, and specific design features.
  • Combining Strength with Speed: Leveraging the structural spanning capabilities of steel for larger commercial or industrial spaces, while integrating Anyplace modules for efficient and adaptable office layouts, meeting rooms, or specialized functional areas.
  • Creating Unique Architectural Expressions: Allowing for innovative designs that combine the clean lines and modularity of Anyplace with the organic forms and textures achievable with timber or the robust framework of steel.

By embracing hybrid construction with Anyplace, we move beyond the limitations of a single material or construction method. This approach allows for optimized material usage, potentially faster project timelines, greater design flexibility, and the ability to tailor buildings precisely to specific needs and aesthetic preferences.
