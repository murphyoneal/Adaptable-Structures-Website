What we respond to

Investment inquiries, partnership conversations, regulatory and certification pathways, government and disaster-response procurement questions, academic research collaboration, and serious journalist inquiries — all welcomed and answered. Sales of the manufactured product are not yet open; the first US production run is in preparation under the Hydro North America agreement.

What this page is not

This page is not a sales channel for the manufactured product. The Anyplace system has been deliberately held out of commercial sale during the patent enforcement period — a strategic decision documented in the company's IP record. Inquiries about pre-purchase, reservation, or commercial procurement will be acknowledged but cannot be fulfilled until commercial release.
